What is Pace Time-Sheet?

Pace Time-Sheet is a convenient and user friendly application that efficiently tracks the amount of time an employee has worked within a certain period of time, whilst maintaining the privacy of its users and creating a steady relationship between the employee and the employers. The app will provide three main features: time tracking and monitoring of work hours, automated maintenance of daily wages and the management and distribution of company documents.
