What is Pace Time-Sheet?

Pace Time-Sheet is a convenient and user friendly application that efficiently tracks the amount of time an employee has worked within a certain period of time, whilst maintaining the privacy of its users and creating a steady relationship between the employee and the employers.

What is our focus?

  • Time tracking and monitoring of work hours
  • Automated maintenance of daily wages
  • Management and distribution of company documents.
